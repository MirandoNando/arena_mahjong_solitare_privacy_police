Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ARENAGO ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use Arena Mahjong Solitaire.

Arena Mahjong Solitaire does not display advertisements and does not sell your personal information. The game uses Firebase services for authentication, account management, cloud save, and online leaderboard functionality.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the game, we may collect:

  • Email address or authentication identifier when you sign in.
  • Guest account identifier if you play as a guest.
  • Nickname or display name.
  • Game progress, including levels, scores, coins, stars, rewards, achievements, and gameplay status.
  • Leaderboard information, including your nickname (or guest display name) and game scores if you choose to participate.
  • Basic technical information processed by Firebase, such as device information, authentication information, and application diagnostics necessary to operate the service.

2. Guest Mode

You may play Arena Mahjong Solitaire as a guest. Guest progress may be stored locally on your device and/or associated with a guest identifier. If you uninstall the application, clear application data, or switch devices, guest progress may be permanently lost unless linked to a registered account.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and authenticate player accounts.
  • Allow guest gameplay.
  • Save and synchronize game progress.
  • Restore saved progress across devices.
  • Provide online leaderboard functionality.
  • Upload your nickname (or guest display name) and game scores to the global leaderboard only after you have given your consent.
  • Maintain security, detect abuse, and improve application stability.

4. Global Leaderboard

Arena Mahjong Solitaire includes an optional global leaderboard that allows players to compare scores with other players worldwide.

If you choose to participate, the game will upload the following information to our server:

  • Your nickname or guest display name.
  • Your game scores.
  • The date and time of the submitted score.

Before your first leaderboard submission, the game will ask for your consent. If you decline, your scores will remain on your device and will not be uploaded to the global leaderboard.

If you later choose to participate, future scores may be uploaded and displayed to other players through the in-game leaderboard.

5. Firebase Services

Arena Mahjong Solitaire uses Firebase, a service provided by Google, for authentication and cloud data storage. Firebase may process information according to Google's Privacy Policy.

For more information, visit:
https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

6. Advertising

Arena Mahjong Solitaire does not display third-party advertisements and does not collect personal information for advertising or marketing purposes.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited information with trusted service providers necessary to operate the game, including Firebase.

If you choose to participate in the online leaderboard, your nickname (or guest display name) and submitted game scores will be visible to other players within the leaderboard.

We may also disclose information when required by law or when necessary to protect our legal rights, users, or services.

8. Data Retention

We retain account information and saved game data only as long as necessary to provide the service. Guest data may remain stored locally until application data is deleted. Account-related cloud data may remain in Firebase until deletion is requested or the account is removed.

9. Account and Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account or personal data by contacting us at:

support@arenago.id

Please provide sufficient information to identify your account.

10. Children's Privacy

Arena Mahjong Solitaire is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children where parental consent is required by applicable law. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will investigate and remove such information where appropriate.

11. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no Internet transmission or electronic storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, the updated version will be posted on this page together with a revised effective date.
