Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ARENAGO ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use Arena Mahjong Soltaire.

Arena Mahjong Soltaire does not display ads and does not sell personal data. The game only uses Firebase services for account creation, authentication, guest access, and game progress storage.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the game, we may collect the following information:

  • Account information, such as email address or authentication identifier, when you create or sign in to an account.
  • Guest account identifier when you play as a guest.
  • Game progress data, such as level, score, coins, stars, rewards, leaderboard-related score, and gameplay status.
  • Basic technical data processed by Firebase, such as device, app, and authentication-related information needed to operate the service.

2. Guest Mode

You can play Arena Mahjong Soltaire as a guest. Guest progress may be stored locally on your device and/or linked to a guest identifier. If you delete the app, clear app data, or change devices, guest progress may be lost unless it has been connected to an account.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and authenticate player accounts.
  • Allow guest access.
  • Save and sync game progress.
  • Restore player data when signing in.
  • Provide leaderboard and score-related features, if available in the game.
  • Maintain security, prevent misuse, and improve game stability.

4. Firebase Services

Arena Mahjong Soltaire uses Firebase, a service provided by Google, for authentication and data storage. Firebase may process information according to Google's own privacy practices. You can learn more at: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5. Advertising

Arena Mahjong Soltaire does not use advertising services, does not show third-party ads, and does not collect data for advertising tracking.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data only with service providers needed to operate the game, such as Firebase. We may also disclose information if required by law or necessary to protect our rights, users, or services.

7. Data Retention

We keep account and game progress data for as long as needed to provide the game service. Guest data may remain on your device until the app data is deleted. Account-related data may remain in Firebase until deletion is requested or the account is removed.

8. Account and Data Deletion

You may request account or data deletion by contacting us at support@arenago.id. Please include enough information for us to identify your account or guest profile.

9. Children's Privacy

Arena Mahjong Soltaire is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children where parental consent is required by applicable law. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and remove it if necessary.

10. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect player information. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
